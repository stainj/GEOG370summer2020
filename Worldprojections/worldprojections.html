In this project I learned how to display images in different projections

I used a vector that contained polygons of all the countries called ne_10m_admin_0_countries and a plugin called indicatrix mapper, then I manually changed the Coordiante Reference System (CRS) to display the map in different projections

WGS84 Projection

Preserves the shape of the countries closer to the equater, but distorts the size closer to the poles

Aitoff Projection

Preserves the area closer to the equater, but distorts the shape of the countries closer to the poles

Pseudo Mercator

Preserves the shape of countries, but distorts the size of the countries closer to the poles

Winkel Tripel

Creates a balance between the size and shape of the countries, but does not show the of distance between countries accuratly

World Cylindrical Equal Area

As the title suggest it preserves the total area, but does not accuratly show the shape of the countries

Conic Projection

The area of the countries is more accurate closer to the north pole and distorts the shape of the countries further away from the north pole

Azimuthal Projection

Preserves the direction and does the the same thing as a conic projection

World Goode Homolosine

Preserves the area closer to the equater then distorts the shape closer to the poles

World Equidistant Cylindrical

As the title suggest for this projection it preserves the distance, but not the countries closer to the poles
